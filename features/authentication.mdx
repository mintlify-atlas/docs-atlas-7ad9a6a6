The authentication system ensures that only authorized users can access your nexDash account. Log in securely with your email and password to view customer data, manage invoices, and track revenue.

## Login process

Access your nexDash dashboard through a secure login form that validates your credentials before granting access.

### Login requirements

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter email address">
    Provide the email address associated with your nexDash account. The email field validates that you enter a properly formatted email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter password">
    Input your account password. Passwords must be at least 6 characters long for security.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit credentials">
    Click the "Log in" button to authenticate. The system verifies your credentials against the user database.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Make sure you enter your credentials correctly. Multiple failed login attempts may trigger security measures.
</Warning>

## User data structure

User accounts in nexDash follow this structure:

```typescript
type User = {
  id: string;
  name: string;
  email: string;
  password: string;
}
```

<Note>
  Passwords are securely hashed in the database. Plain text passwords are never stored.
</Note>

## Authentication workflow

Understand how nexDash verifies your identity:

```typescript
export async function getUser(email: string) {
  const user = await sql`SELECT * FROM users WHERE email=${email}`;
  return user.rows[0] as User;
}
```

<Steps>
  <Step title="Form submission">
    When you click "Log in", your email and password are sent to the authentication system
  </Step>
  <Step title="User lookup">
    The system queries the database to find a user account matching your email address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Password verification">
    Your entered password is compared with the hashed password stored in the database
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access granted or denied">
    If credentials match, you're logged in and redirected to the dashboard. If not, an error is displayed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security features

nexDash implements several security measures to protect your account:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Password requirements">
    Passwords must be at least 6 characters long. While this is the minimum, we recommend using:
    - A mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
    - Numbers and special characters
    - Unique passwords not used on other sites
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Password hashing">
    User passwords are never stored in plain text. They are hashed using secure one-way encryption, making it impossible to retrieve the original password.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Email validation">
    The login form validates email format before submission, preventing common typos and ensuring proper email structure.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Required fields">
    Both email and password are required fields. The form cannot be submitted with missing credentials.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting login issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Forgot password">
    If you've forgotten your password, contact your nexDash administrator to reset your account credentials. Password reset functionality may be available depending on your installation.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Invalid email or password">
    If you receive an error after submitting:
    - Double-check your email address for typos
    - Verify your password is correct (remember it's case-sensitive)
    - Ensure you're using the correct account credentials
    - Check if Caps Lock is enabled
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Account not found">
    If the system can't find your account:
    - Verify you're using the email address registered with nexDash
    - Contact your administrator to confirm your account exists
    - Check if your account may have been deactivated
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Form validation errors">
    If the form won't submit:
    - Ensure email is in valid format (user@domain.com)
    - Check that password meets minimum length of 6 characters
    - Verify both fields are filled in
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Session management

Once authenticated, your session remains active while using nexDash:

- **Persistent session** - Stay logged in as you navigate between pages
- **Automatic routing** - After login, you're redirected to the dashboard
- **Protected routes** - Attempting to access pages without logging in redirects to login form

<Warning>
  For security, always log out when using shared computers or devices. Close your browser when finished to end your session.
</Warning>

## User account information

After logging in, nexDash knows your identity and can:

- Display your name in the interface
- Track your actions and changes
- Personalize your dashboard experience
- Associate data modifications with your user account
