The customer management feature provides a comprehensive view of all your customers, their contact information, and their invoice history. You can search for specific customers and track their payment activity at a glance.

## Viewing customers

The customers page displays all customers in your nexDash account in a searchable table format.

<Note>
  Customer data includes profile images, contact details, and aggregated invoice metrics for quick financial insights.
</Note>

### Customer information displayed

Each customer entry shows:

- **Name and profile image** - Visual identification of the customer
- **Email address** - Primary contact information
- **Total invoices** - Count of all invoices associated with this customer
- **Total pending** - Sum of all unpaid invoices in USD
- **Total paid** - Sum of all completed payments in USD

### Data structure

Customer records follow this structure:

```typescript
type CustomersTableType = {
  id: string;
  name: string;
  email: string;
  image_url: string;
  total_invoices: number;
  total_pending: number;
  total_paid: number;
}
```

## Searching customers

Use the search bar at the top of the customers page to filter your customer list.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter search term">
    Type a customer name or email address in the search field
  </Step>
  <Step title="View filtered results">
    The table automatically updates to show matching customers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear search">
    Remove your search term to display all customers again
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What you can search

The search functionality filters customers by:

- Customer name (case-insensitive)
- Email address (case-insensitive)

<Tip>
  Use partial matches to find customers quickly. For example, searching "john" will find "John Smith" and "johnny@example.com"
</Tip>

## Customer table views

nexDash adapts the customer display based on your screen size:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop view">
    On larger screens, customers appear in a full table with columns for:
    - Name (with profile image)
    - Email
    - Total Invoices
    - Total Pending
    - Total Paid

    This view makes it easy to scan across multiple customers and compare metrics.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile view">
    On mobile devices, each customer appears as a card showing:
    - Profile image and name at the top
    - Email address below the name
    - Pending and paid amounts in a two-column layout
    - Total invoice count at the bottom

    Cards are stacked vertically for easy scrolling on smaller screens.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Understanding customer metrics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How are invoice totals calculated?">
    The total invoices count includes all invoices linked to a customer, regardless of payment status. This gives you a complete picture of your business relationship.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What does 'total pending' mean?">
    Total pending represents the sum of all invoices with a `pending` status for this customer. These are invoices that have been created but not yet marked as paid.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What does 'total paid' include?">
    Total paid is the sum of all invoices marked with a `paid` status. This shows how much revenue you've successfully collected from this customer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
